# User Personas

## Persona 1: Sarah, 28-year-old Individual
- **Background**: Recent graduate, starting career, wants to build good financial habits and personal goals
- **Goals**: Save for emergency fund, develop daily exercise habit, plan career advancement
- **Pain Points**: Overwhelmed by options, needs simple tracking
- **Use Case**: Set monthly savings goal, track daily habits, view progress dashboard

## Persona 2: The Johnson Family
- **Background**: Married couple with 2 kids, middle-income, planning for children's education and retirement
- **Goals**: Family budget, college savings, shared chores and habits
- **Pain Points**: Coordinating family goals, tracking shared expenses
- **Use Case**: Create family accounts, set joint goals, assign tasks to members

## Persona 3: Mark, 45-year-old Professional
- **Background**: Established career, high income, wants to optimize investments and plan for retirement
- **Goals**: Maximize retirement savings, assess financial risks, maintain work-life balance
- **Pain Points**: Complex financial planning, time management
- **Use Case**: Financial projections, risk assessment tools, long-term planning
